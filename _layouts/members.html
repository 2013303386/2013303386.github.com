Faculty

Dr. Chen Li serves as a professor in the computer science department in Xi'an Jiaotong University and leads the Biomedical Semantic Understanding Group (BioSun). Before joining the university, He had been working in the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology as a research scientist after receiving PhD from the University of Cambridge in England. While in Cambridge, his project was selected to be done in the European Bioinformatics Institute.
